# Privacy Policy - Wapp

**Last updated:** April 13, 2026

Wapp is a free Android application (mobile and Android TV) that allows users to find and watch anime. 

**Important:** Wapp does **not** host, upload, store, or provide any anime content on its own servers. All video streams and links are provided by third-party external websites.

## 1. Information We Collect

Wapp does not collect any personal information from its users. We do not require account registration, email, phone number, or any other personal data to use the app.

The only information that may be collected automatically is:

- Device information (device model, operating system version, screen resolution, etc.)
- Advertising ID (Google Advertising ID)
- Basic usage data (which screens are opened, session duration)

This information is used solely to show advertisements and improve the app experience.

## 2. Third-Party Services

Wapp uses third-party advertising networks (such as Google AdMob, AppLovin, or others) to display video advertisements. These services may collect certain information as described in their own privacy policies, including your Advertising ID and IP address to deliver relevant ads.

Wapp also redirects users to external third-party websites for anime streaming. We have no control over the content, privacy practices, or security of those external websites. We strongly recommend that you review the privacy policies of any third-party site you visit through the app.

## 3. Permissions Requested

The app requires the following permissions:
- Internet access (to load anime links and streams)
- Network state (to check internet connection)

No other sensitive permissions are requested.

## 4. Children's Privacy

Wapp is intended for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

## 5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the app and the date of the last update will be modified at the top of this page.
